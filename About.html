About us

Born from a shared passion for plants, Lucky Shrub was founded by Jason and Maria. Jason, our visionary garden architect, brings landscapes to life through meticulous design and skilled execution. Maria, with a keen eye for aesthetics and a flair for communication, oversees our marketing and nursery operations.

Our journey began with a deep-rooted love for the transformative power of nature. We believe that every outdoor space holds the potential to become a serene sanctuary or a vibrant masterpiece. With a team of dedicated horticulturists and skilled craftsmen, we strive to turn these potentials into reality.

At the heart of Lucky Shrub is a commitment to understanding our clients' unique visions. We believe in collaboration, open communication, and a meticulous attention to detail. Our goal is to create outdoor spaces that not only meet but exceed your expectations, transforming your property into a beloved extension of your home.

Company Mission

Our company's mission is to transform outdoor spaces into captivating and harmonious landscapes that inspire and enrich the lives of our clients. We believe that well-designed landscapes can create meaningful connections between people and nature. With a focus on exceptional craftsmanship and personalized service, we collaborate closely with clients to understand their visions and deliver innovative design solutions.

Sustainability is integral to our mission, as we embrace eco-friendly practices and promote environmental stewardship. By fostering long-lasting relationships and exceeding expectations, we aim to enhance the overall quality of life for our clients and create extraordinary outdoor havens that celebrate nature's beauty.
