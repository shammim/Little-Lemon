About Us

Lucky Shrub was started by Jason and Maria, who share a long-time love for plants. Jason is the garden architect. He creates and oversees all designs while managing his team of landscapers. Maria manages all the marketing for the company and oversees the nursery.

Since our establishment, we have nurtured a deep appreciation for the beauty and serenity that well-designed landscapes can bring to both residential and commercial properties. Our team of experienced and dedicated landscape designers, horticulturists, and craftsmen work tirelessly to transform outdoor spaces into breathtaking havens that reflect the unique vision and preferences of our clients.

We pride ourselves on our commitment to quality craftsmanship, attention to detail, and personalized customer service. We believe that successful landscaping projects are built on a foundation of collaboration and understanding. That's why we take the time to listen to our clients, understand their desires, and translate their dreams into reality.

Company Mission

Our company's mission is to transform outdoor spaces into captivating and harmonious landscapes that inspire and enrich the lives of our clients. We believe that well-designed landscapes can create meaningful connections between people and nature. With a focus on exceptional craftsmanship and personalized service, we collaborate closely with clients to understand their visions and deliver innovative design solutions.

Sustainability is integral to our mission, as we embrace eco-friendly practices and promote environmental stewardship. By fostering long-lasting relationships and exceeding expectations, we aim to enhance the overall quality of life for our clients and create extraordinary outdoor havens that celebrate nature's beauty.
