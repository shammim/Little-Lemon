Hardscaping Projects


Elevated Entertaining Haven

We created a stunning entertaining area for a family who loves to host. We installed a spacious flagstone patio perfect for large gatherings. Retaining walls were strategically placed to create a multi-level space, offering visual interest and maximizing usable area. Built-in benches with comfortable seating provided ample room for guests, while a custom-built outdoor kitchen with a grill and bar area made entertaining a breeze. This project showcased how hardscaping elements can create a functional and inviting space for unforgettable gatherings.

Modern Minimalist Masterpiece

For a client who craved a clean and modern aesthetic, we designed a minimalist masterpiece. We installed sleek concrete walkways that weaved through the property, creating a sense of flow and guiding guests through the space. A modern fire pit with a geometric concrete surround became the focal point, adding both style and warmth. We incorporated low-maintenance gravel gardens around these hardscape elements, further emphasizing the minimalist theme and reducing the need for extensive plant care. This project demonstrates how Lucky Shrub can use modern hardscaping to create a sophisticated and low-maintenance outdoor haven.

Rustic Retreat with a View

We transformed a rustic cabin's outdoor space to complement its natural surroundings. We built a natural stone patio that seamlessly blended with the existing landscape. A winding pathway made of flagstone guided guests through the property, providing a charming approach to the cabin. We incorporated a custom-built stone fire pit perfect for cozy evenings under the stars. This project highlights how Lucky Shrub can use natural materials and hardscaping elements to create a rustic retreat that enhances the beauty of its natural environment.

Urban Oasis with a Water Feature

For a city dweller yearning for a tranquil escape, we designed an urban oasis. We installed a beautiful flagstone patio with a custom-built water feature that added a touch of serenity and the soothing sound of trickling water. We built raised garden beds edged with natural stone, allowing for planting a variety of flowers and herbs. This project demonstrates Lucky Shrub's ability to use hardscaping elements to create a peaceful retreat even in a limited urban space.
